Suggestions for different ways you could use the practice exercises:

  • Click on the arrow and listen as each item is spoken
  • Pronounce each item and click on the item to check
  • Click on the arrow and say each item with the audio

With the drag-and-drop exercises, be sure to drag and drop the last item (there's a small "reward").

Hints

When ֨ ⁣ is before the last letter in a word, this indicates the first letter of the stressed syllable.
When ֙ ◌ is at the end of a word, and there is no other ֨ ⁣ in the word, the last syllable is stressed.
